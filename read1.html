Smart city and Cleanest city Indore

Indore is the most populous and largest city in the indian state of Madhy-Pradesh. Indore has been selected as one of the 100 indian cities to be developed as a smart city under the Smart cities mission. Indore has been ranked as india's Cleanest city five years in a row as per the Swachch Survekshan for the years 2017,2018,2019,2020 and 2021. Meanwhile indore has also been decalred as india's First "Water plus" city under the 'Swachch Survekshan' 2021.

languages:-

Hindi is the Official language of the indore city, a number of Hindi dialects such as Malvi,Nimadi and Bundeli are spoken in decent number.

Food:-

The Street food of the city is especially popular Two of the most street food places in indore are 'Chappan Dukan' and 'Sarafa Bazar'.

About indore:-

  • Rajwada at indore is the only 7 storeyed entrance of the palace.
  • 40 feet high idol at "Bada Ganapati" is the largest Ganesh ji idol in the world.
  • Indore has a huge concrete Cricket Bat statue with names of the players of indian team which won the 1971 series againest west-indies team. there many not be many of its kind in the world.
  • Indore is the only city in india to have an "indian institute of management" and "Indian institute of technology"
  • Pitra parvat Hanumaan Temple is one of the biggest metal statue of Lord 'Hanuman ji' in indore (M.P). it the tallest statue of Hanuman sitting in the country. This temple also known as "Shree Pitreshwar Hanuman Dham Mandir".

Festivals:-

owing to its rich cultural background, indore festivals are celebrated with great zest and are one of the main attractions of the city.All national festivals like Diwali, Holi,Eid and Rakhi are also celebrated in indore with the same enthusiasm as in the other parts of Madhy-Pradesh as well india.
